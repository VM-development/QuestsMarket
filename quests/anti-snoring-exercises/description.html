The most important component of snoring treatment is exercises to train the muscles of the palate and jaw, pharynx, and larynx, which give very good results. According to many people who have recovered from snoring, the most effective are precisely such exercises and breathing exercises, writes gospoda.com.ua.

The following set of exercises is considered by many doctors to be the most effective. By doing them for a month, you will feel how snoring has significantly decreased, and in the future, you will completely get rid of it.

  1. Push your tongue forward and down as far as possible — you should feel the tension of the pharyngeal muscles near the very root of the tongue. Hold in the extended state for 1–2 seconds and at the same time pronounce the long sound "i". Do it twice a day, 30 times. This exercise will strengthen the muscles of the uvula, palate and tongue.
  2. Press your chin with your hand, move your lower jaw forward and backward with effort. Do this exercise twice a day, 30 times. This trains the muscles of the lower jaw, which push it forward, which helps to increase the pharyngeal lumen and reduce snoring, and serves as a prevention of apnea.
  3. Strongly clench a stick — wooden or plastic (pencil, pen) — with your teeth, hold it for up to 3–4 minutes. Do it before bedtime. This exercise trains the pharyngeal and chewing muscles, reducing snoring at the beginning of falling asleep, which will help the snorer's loved ones: they have the opportunity to fall asleep in silence.
  4. Open your mouth slightly, make 10–15 clockwise and counterclockwise movements with your lower jaw.
  5. Whistling exercises the larynx and palate muscles. When walking in the fresh air, try to breathe whistling. At the same time, your head should be raised high, your shoulders straightened, and your steps should be the same length. Exhale for 6–7 steps, while whistling your favorite melody. Whistle in this way for your health for 25 minutes every day.
  6. Repeat the sound "i" with effort 20–30 times — it is even better to sing this sound. This trains the muscles of the uvula and the walls of the pharynx. It is also useful to pronounce the sound "i" for a long time and sing it. After a month of such singing, your loved ones may be able to sleep in complete silence, because you will get rid of snoring.
  7. Exercise to strengthen the throat. Press your tongue firmly against the roof of your mouth. Do the exercise until you feel tired.
  8. Exercises to strengthen the upper soft palate. Close your mouth, breathe through your nose. Pull your tongue back with force towards your throat, repeat 10–15 times. Do this 2–3 times daily.

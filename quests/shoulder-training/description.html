A dumbbell session built around the shoulders, with supporting leg, back and core work. Do the exercises in order, resting 60–90 seconds between sets. 🎯 marks the main shoulder movers.

1. Goblet squat — 3 × 10

Hold one dumbbell vertically against your chest. Sit down between your hips, keeping your chest tall and your knees tracking over your toes.

2. Overhead press 🎯 — 3 × 8–10

From shoulder height, press the dumbbells overhead until your arms are straight. Brace your core and keep your ribs down so you don't arch your lower back.

3. Bent-over row — 3 × 10

Hinge at the hips with a flat back and soft knees. Row the dumbbells toward your waist, squeezing your shoulder blades together at the top.

4. Lateral raise 🎯 — 3 × 12–15

With a slight bend in the elbows, raise the dumbbells out to your sides up to shoulder height. Lead with your elbows, not your hands, and lower under control.

5. Floor press — 3 × 10

Lie on your back with knees bent and upper arms resting on the floor. Press the dumbbells up and slightly together over your chest, then lower until your triceps touch down.

6. Plank — 3 × 20–30 sec

Forearms under your shoulders, body in a straight line from head to heels. Brace your abs and glutes and breathe steadily — don't let your hips sag or pike up.

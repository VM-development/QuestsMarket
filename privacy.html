Golden Grace — Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 20, 2026

Golden Grace is a habit-tracking game designed to be private by default. This policy explains what data the app handles.

Data stored on your device

By default, everything you create in Golden Grace — your quests, completions, stats, character level, pet, and settings — is stored locally on your device. We do not collect or transmit this data, and no account is required to use the app.

Optional sign-in and social features

Golden Grace includes optional social features (friends, dares, and a leaderboard) available only if you choose to use Sign in with Apple. If you sign in, the following is sent to and stored on our server solely to provide those features:

  • A unique account identifier provided by Apple
  • An email address (which may be Apple’s private relay) if you choose to share it
  • A display name you set
  • Your character level and aggregate stats, to power friends and the leaderboard

We use this data only to operate the social features. We do not sell it and do not use it for advertising.

What we don’t do

  • No third-party advertising
  • No third-party analytics or tracking SDKs
  • We do not track you across other apps or websites

Quests Market

The in-app Quests Market loads publicly available, community-contributed quest content from a public repository. Browsing it sends us no personal information.

Data retention and deletion

  • Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data from your device.
  • If you used sign-in and want your server-side data deleted, email us and we will delete it.

Children

Golden Grace is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect their personal information.

Changes

We may update this policy; the “Last updated” date above reflects any changes.
